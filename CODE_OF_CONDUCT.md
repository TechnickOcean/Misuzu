## English

### Our Commitment

We are committed to providing a welcoming and inclusive environment for all people, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, sex, sexual identity and orientation, socioeconomic status, or any other dimension of identity.

### Our Standards

We value the participation of every member of the community and want to ensure that every contributor has an enjoyable and fulfilling experience. Accordingly, all members are expected to show respect and courtesy to every other community member.

**Include in this list actions that contribute to creating a positive environment:**

- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy towards other community members
- Being patient and kind to those new to the project
- Encouraging and supporting other community members

**Examples of unacceptable behavior include:**

- The use of sexualized language or imagery and unwelcome sexual attention or advances
- Trolling, insulting/derogatory comments, and personal or political attacks
- Public or private harassment
- Publishing others' private information, such as a physical or electronic address, without explicit permission
- Other conduct which could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a professional setting
- Discrimination based on any characteristic or protected status
- Intimidation or bullying (online or in-person)

### Enforcement

#### Reporting

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior, or have any other concerns, please report it by contacting the project maintainers at `conduct@example.com`. All reports will be kept confidential.

**When reporting, please include:**

- Your contact information
- Identifying information about the incident
- Description of what happened
- Names of witnesses (if any)
- Any relevant links, screenshots, or other documentation

#### Investigation

- We will review all complaints promptly and fairly
- The investigation will be thorough and confidential
- We will contact all relevant parties
- We will keep the reporter informed of progress
- We aim to resolve issues within 2 weeks

#### Resolution

Possible resolutions include:

- Warning the person responsible
- Requiring an apology
- Temporary suspension from community participation
- Permanent removal from the community
- Referral to appropriate authorities (if necessary)

The specific action taken will depend on the severity and nature of the violation.

### Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to:

- All project spaces (GitHub, discussions, Discord, etc.)
- In-person events organized by the project
- Communications representing the project
- Interactions between community members related to the project

This Code of Conduct is adapted from the [Contributor Covenant](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/), version 2.1, available at https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/

## Chinese

### 我们的承诺

我们承诺为所有人创造一个欢迎和包容的环境，无论年龄、身体状况、残疾、种族、性别认同和表达、经验水平、国籍、个人外表、种族、宗教、性别、性取向或任何其他身份特征如何。

### 我们的标准

我们重视社区每个成员的参与，希望确保每个贡献者都有愉快和充实的体验。因此，所有成员都应该对每个其他社区成员表现出尊重和礼貌。

**包括在内有助于创建积极环境的行为：**

- 使用欢迎和包容的语言
- 尊重不同的观点和经历
- 优雅地接受建设性批评
- 关注对社区最有利的事项
- 对其他社区成员表现同情
- 对项目新手表现耐心和善良
- 鼓励和支持其他社区成员

**不可接受行为的例子包括：**

- 使用性化语言或意象以及不受欢迎的性注意或性骚扰
- 骚扰、侮辱/贬低性评论和个人或政治攻击
- 公开或私下骚扰
- 未经明确许可发布他人私人信息，如物理或电子地址
- 在专业环境中合理认为可能不适当的其他行为
- 基于任何特征或受保护地位的歧视
- 恐吓或欺凌（在线或线下）

### 执行

#### 报告

如果你经历或目睹不可接受的行为，或有任何其他顾虑，请通过联系项目维护者 `conduct@example.com` 来报告。所有报告将被保密。

**报告时，请包括：**

- 你的联系方式
- 事件的识别信息
- 发生了什么的说明
- 证人的名字（如有）
- 任何相关的链接、截图或其他文件

#### 调查

- 我们会迅速公平地审查所有投诉
- 调查将是彻底和保密的
- 我们将联系所有相关方
- 我们将告知记者的进展情况
- 我们的目标是在 2 周内解决问题

#### 解决

可能的解决方案包括：

- 警告责任人
- 要求道歉
- 临时禁止参加社区活动
- 永久从社区中移除
- 向相关当局转介（如有必要）

具体采取的行动将取决于违规的严重程度和性质。

### 适用范围

本行为准则适用于：

- 所有项目空间（GitHub、讨论、Discord 等）
- 项目组织的线下活动
- 代表项目的通信
- 社区成员之间与项目相关的互动
