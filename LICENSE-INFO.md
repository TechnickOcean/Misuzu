### Common Misconceptions

**Myth**: "GPL means I have to give away my application for free"

**Truth**: GPL doesn't restrict pricing. You can charge for your application. But users must have access to the source code and the freedom to modify it.

---

**Myth**: "I can't use GPL software in a commercial project"

**Truth**: You can use GPL software commercially. But if you distribute your application, it must also be GPL-licensed.

---

**Myth**: "GPL is viral and will infect my code"

**Truth**: GPL only applies to code you've actually modified or included. Linking to GPL libraries in separate processes may not trigger GPL requirements (depends on interpretation).

**误解**："GPL 意味着我必须免费提供我的应用程序"

**事实**：GPL 不限制定价。您可以为您的应用程序收费。但用户必须能够访问源代码并且可以自由修改它。

---

**误解**："我不能在商业项目中使用 GPL 软件"

**事实**：您可以在商业上使用 GPL 软件。但如果您分发您的应用程序，它也必须获得 GPL 许可。

---

**误解**："GPL 具有病毒性，会感染我的代码"

**事实**：GPL 仅适用于您实际修改或包含的代码。在单独进程中链接到 GPL 库可能不会触发 GPL 要求（取决于解释）。
